How It Works

01

Image Preprocessing

Your image is resized to 224×224 pixels and normalized to a [0,1] tensor compatible with MobileNetV2 input requirements.

02

CNN Feature Extraction

MobileNetV2's depthwise separable convolutions extract hierarchical features — edges, textures, shapes, and high-level semantics.

03

Softmax Classification

The final dense layer with 1000 outputs applies Softmax to produce probability distributions across all ImageNet categories.

04

Ranked Predictions

Top-5 predictions are returned with confidence scores, helping you understand both the primary classification and alternative interpretations.
